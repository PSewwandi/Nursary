 Beginning…

Weems Creek Nursery School took root by founder and Annapolis residents Rod and Missy Tate in 1972. Mrs. Tate's philosophy was to nurture young learners while developing social skills and a sense of one self. And since it's beginning, nestled in the heart of West Annapolis, Weems Creek Nursery School & Kindergarten has been providing a nurturing, engaging, creative learning experience for over 40 years.

Admissions Process

At Weems Creek, the admissions process typically begins with a tour of the school, either privately or at one of our Open Houses. Once a family has determined they wish to apply to Weems, the procedure is as follows: Complete application form. Submit the form and the application fee to the school's director. Upon review of the application, families will receive an emailed letter with the outcome of the school's decision regarding acceptance. Upon acceptance, it is incumbent on the family to pay a $500 deposit* to secure the student's enrollment. At this time, families will receive further information to set up the preferred payment method and payment plan as well as receive initial paperwork required for enrollment. * Tuition available upon request.
